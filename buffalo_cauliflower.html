Buffalo Cauliflower

I originally discovered this recipe in a reddit thread. The link to the original recipe source is in that thread and can be found here. This makes an excellent vegetarian-friendly alternative to chicken wings and the texture is as close to perfect as it gets.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower; chopped into bite size pieces
  • 1/2 cup of brown rice flour or regular flour
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • Pinch of kosher salt
  • Pinch of granulated garlic powder
  • Non-stick spray

Sauce

  • 1 tsp. Earth Balance butter substitute or real butter; melted
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450Â°F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine flour, water, garlic powder, and salt. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Dip cauliflower in batter then place onto lightly greased, non-stick baking sheet.
  4. Bake about 10 minutes, or until batter hardens. Flip and bake another 5 minutes.
  5. Whisk hot sauce and butter in a small bowl.
  6. Brush cauliflower with sauce.
  7. Bake an additional 8-10 minutes or until cauliflower is crispy.
