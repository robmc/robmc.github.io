Honey Balsamic Carrots

This is a simple side dish that I like. It brings a great flavor to the carrots.

Ingredients

  • Rainbow carrots
  • 6 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parsley (for garnish)

Ingredients organized on stovetop

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Wash and peel the carrots.Slice thicker carrots in half to get an even cook.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt and pepper. Toss the carrots in the mixture to evenly coat them. Place on an oiled baking sheet. I covered my baking sheet in foil since the mixture has honey and I didn't want anything to burn on the sheet.

    Peeled carrots beside mixing bowl


    Carrots on baking sheet covered with mixture
  4. Roast the carrots until tender. This should take about 30-35 minutes.
