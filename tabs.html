Page Tabs

This example was taken from the ARIA Authoring Practices. It is the Example of Tabs with Automatic Activation. I gave it a "dark mode" appearance but, other than that, it's their code. They've documented the keyboard support on their page. I didn't want to reinvent the wheel, I just wanted an example I could easily style and understand. For more information about the roles and structures used and expected behavior of this widget, follow the aforementioned link.
