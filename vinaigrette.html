Vinaigrette

Combine ingredients into mason jar or tupperware container. Recipe lasts about 7-10 days before ingredients separate and thicken.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 3 Tbsp vinegar of choice (Balsamic, Red Wine, White Wine)
  • 1 Tbsp dijon mustard (recommend coarse ground dijon mustard)
  • 2 medium garlic cloves, pressed or minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • [Optional] 1 Tbsp maple syrup or honey
